HOW TO PLAY


YOUR GOAL IS TO REACH SPACE STATION AND SELL ALL
VALUABLES IN YOUR CARGO.
PROGRESS BARS AT THE TOP INDICATE SPACE LEFT IN YOUR
CARGO BAY AND DISTANCE TO SPACE STATION.
EVERYTHING YOU PICK UP WILL DECREASE THAT FREE SPACE.
SOME THINGS YOU CAN PICK UP:
- IMPORT VALUABLES THAT SELL FOR $
- SPEED AND POWER AUGMENTS
- BOMBS... PROBABLY NOT A GOOD IDEA
ON YOUR WAY YOU MIGHT ALSO ENCOUNTER SOME DIFFICULTIES.
TO OVERCOME THEM USE ARROW KEYS TO MOVE AND SPACE BAR TO

BLAST'EM!
